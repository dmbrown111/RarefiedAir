Getting Certified:

In order to get a USPA liciense you must:
> Complete a minimum of 25 jumps.
> Complete all requirements laid out by the USPA License Proficiency Card.
> Make five skidives with one or more people.
> Have skydiving license stamped by the USPA.
> Pass USPA written and oral exams.

Length of Jump:

Most dives last approximately 2 hours, which includes paperwork, training, equipment preparation, aircraft ride,
the jump itself, and removal of equipment. We cannot guarantee flight conditions or specify exact times for the
day of your jump. Be prepared to wait up to 4 hours if needed.
